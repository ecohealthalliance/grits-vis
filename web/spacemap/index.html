This visualization embeds disease reports in a multi-dimensional space. The blue dots represent each report. Other colored dots are constraints, which "pull" the reports to different concrete or abstract spaces.

If a map constraint is specified, it will be tied to the underlying map and pull reports to their geolocation. Similarly other constraints could be mapped to the horizontal or vertical axis, or may be unconstrained and simply tend to pull together reports having a common property value.
